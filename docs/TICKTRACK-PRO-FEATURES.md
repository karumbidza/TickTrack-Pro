## 🎯 Core Features

### 1. Intelligent Ticket Management

**The Problem:** Maintenance requests get lost in emails, WhatsApp messages, or verbal communications. There's no way to track progress or hold anyone accountable.

**Our Solution:**

- **One-Click Ticket Creation** - Users can log issues in seconds with photos and descriptions
- **Automatic Ticket Numbering** - Every ticket gets a unique tracking number (e.g., TKT-2026-0001)
- **Smart Routing** - Tickets automatically assigned based on category, location, or priority
- **Status Tracking** - Real-time visibility: Open → Processing → In Progress → Completed
- **SLA Monitoring** - Track response times and ensure service level agreements are met
- **Priority Levels** - Urgent, High, Medium, Low with visual indicators
- **Attachment Support** - Photos, documents, and videos for better issue documentation

**Workflow States:**
```
OPEN → AWAITING_QUOTE → QUOTE_SUBMITTED → PROCESSING → 
ACCEPTED → IN_PROGRESS → ON_SITE → AWAITING_WORK_APPROVAL → 
COMPLETED → CLOSED
```

### 2. Comprehensive Asset Management

**The Problem:** Organizations don't know what assets they have, where they are, or when they need maintenance. This leads to unexpected breakdowns and costly emergency repairs.

**Our Solution:**

- **Complete Asset Register** - Track all assets: vehicles, equipment, machinery, IT hardware, furniture
- **Custom Asset Categories** - Create categories specific to your business (Vehicles, Fuel Tanks, HVAC, etc.)
- **Asset Lifecycle Tracking** - From acquisition to decommissioning
- **QR Code Integration** - Scan to view asset details or create tickets instantly
- **Maintenance History** - Full service history for every asset
- **Location Tracking** - Know where every asset is located across branches
- **Depreciation Tracking** - Monitor asset value over time
- **Warranty Management** - Never miss a warranty claim

**Asset Statuses:**
- ✅ Active
- 🔧 Maintenance
- ⚠️ Repair Needed
- 🚫 Out of Service
- 📦 Transferred
- 🗑️ Decommissioned

### 4. Contractor Network Management

**The Problem:** Finding reliable contractors is difficult. There's no way to verify their credentials, track their performance, or ensure quality work.

**Our Solution:**

#### Contractor Onboarding
- **Invitation System** - Invite contractors via email
- **Self-Registration** - Contractors complete their own profiles
- **KYC Verification** - Upload and verify business documents:
  - Business registration certificates
  - Tax clearance
  - Insurance documents
  - Professional certifications
  - Bank account details

#### Contractor Categories
- Assign contractors to specific service categories
- Electricians, Plumbers, HVAC, IT Support, Cleaning, Security, etc.
- Smart matching: Right contractor for the right job

#### Performance Tracking
- **5-Star Rating System** - Users rate completed work
- **Rating Categories:**
  - Overall satisfaction
  - Punctuality
  - Customer service
  - Workmanship quality
  - PPE compliance
  - Site procedures compliance
- **Performance Reports** - Identify top and underperforming contractors

### 5. Quote & Cost Management

**The Problem:** Repair costs spiral out of control. There's no visibility on quotes, no approval process, and invoices pile up without verification.

**Our Solution:**

#### Quote Workflow
1. **Request Quote** - Admin requests quote from contractor
2. **Quote Submission** - Contractor submits itemized quote
3. **Admin Review** - Compare quotes, negotiate if needed
4. **Approval/Rejection** - Documented decision trail
5. **Work Authorization** - Only approved quotes proceed

#### Invoice Management
- **Invoice Submission** - Contractors submit invoices with supporting documents
- **Proof of Work** - Attach photos of completed work
- **Multi-Level Approval** - Configurable approval workflows
- **Invoice Tracking** - Pending, Approved, Paid, Rejected
- **Payment Batching** - Group invoices for efficient processing
- **Cost Analysis** - Track spending by category, contractor, branch

**The Problem:** Sensitive business data needs to be protected. Organizations need to meet compliance requirements and maintain audit trails.

  - Session management with auto-timeout

  - Email/password authentication
  - Password strength requirements
  - Account activation via email
  - Password reset with OTP
  - Session timeout (8 hours)

- **Audit Trail**
  - All actions logged with timestamps
  - User activity tracking
  - Change history on tickets and assets

- **Data Isolation**
  - Multi-tenant architecture
  - Complete data separation between organizations
  - No cross-tenant data access
